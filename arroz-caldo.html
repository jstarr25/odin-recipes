Arroz Caldo

Description

Arroz Caldo is a traditional Filipino dish that is easy to make and delicious. It is a rice soup that is made with chicken broth, rice, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil the rice and chicken broth
  2. Add the vegetables and cook until the vegetables are tender
  3. Serve hot
