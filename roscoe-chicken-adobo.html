Roscoe Chicken Adobo

Description

This chicken adobo is incredibly easy to make. Chicken simmers in a soy and vinegar sauce with some savory aromatics, and emerges tender and moist.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into parts
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion, chunked
  • 5 leaf bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 8 black peppercorns
  • steamed rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken, working in batches to avoid overcrowding, and cook until browned on all sides, about 2 minutes per batch. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
  2. Add the garlic, onion, bay leaves, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken back to the pot and cook for another 30 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken and bay leaves, then shred the meat.
  5. Serve the chicken over steamed rice.
